# Dutch Golden Age in Painting (Netherland Golden Century)

## Movements

- **Haarlem Mannerism** (c. 1580 - 1600)
- **Pre-Rembrandtists** (c. 1600 - 1620)
- **Dutch Caravaggisti** (c. 1620 - 1630)
- **Delft School** (c. 1650 - 1670)

## Artefact Types

- History Painting
- Portraiture
- Genre Painting
- Landscape Painting

## Places

- Haarlem
- Amsterdam
- Delft
- Leiden

## Artists

- **Frans Hals** (d. 1666)
- **Rembrandt van Rijn** (d. 1669)
- **Johannes Vermeer** (d. 1675)
- **Jan Steen** (d. 1679)
- **Pieter de Hooch** (d. 1684)
- **Jacob van Ruisdael** (d. 1682)

## Influences

- Haarlem Mannerism -> Pre-Rembrandtists
- Pre-Rembrandtists -> Rembrandt van Rijn
- Dutch Caravaggisti -> Rembrandt van Rijn
- Haarlem Mannerism -> Frans Hals
- Frans Hals -> Jan Steen
- Rembrandt van Rijn -> Delft School
- Delft School -> Johannes Vermeer
- Delft School -> Pieter de Hooch
